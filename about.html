Cut Create is a small tight knit studio, that brings years of experience in editing, post production and motion graphics. With backgrounds in film and television, we approach each project with a deep appreciation for the art of storytelling and visual aesthetics.

We collaborate with clients from around the world. We take great pride in working with international clients, understanding the diverse cultural nuances and preferences that enrich our creative process. From multinational corporations to independent creators, our ability to adapt and deliver exceptional results knows no bounds.












Based in South Korea and New Zealand, our studio draws inspiration from these environments. Seoul's fast-paced urban landscape and Christchurch's natural beauty infuse our work with a unique blend of creativity and innovation.

We are truly privileged to operate at the heart of these distinct cultures, and we look forward to bringing this global perspective into your next video project.
